About

I'm a final-year Computer Science Engineering student at SRM University, Sonepat, with a strong foundation in Data Structures & Algorithms and software engineering. My work spans Android development in Kotlin and Java, full-stack systems on the MERN stack, and applied machine learning.

I like problems with a physical shape — routes, signals, safety, supply chains — and I build end-to-end: from system design and data models to the UI someone actually taps on. Most recently that meant shipping SafeTour AI, a location-based safety app, solo, from architecture to release.

Outside coursework, I compete in hackathons and run independent builds, currently in early development on ChainTrack, a role-based supply chain platform.
